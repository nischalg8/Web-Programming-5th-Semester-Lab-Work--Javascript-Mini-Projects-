Why Learn Web Development?

Web development is one of the most important and in-demand skills in the modern technology industry. Almost every business today requires an online presence through websites or web applications.

Learning web development helps students understand how the internet works and how users interact with digital systems. It also improves problem-solving and logical thinking skills.

Web development provides career opportunities in front-end, back-end, and full-stack development. It also enables individuals to build personal projects, portfolios, and startups.

With continuous advancements in web technologies, web development remains a future-proof and highly valuable skill.
